Batman: The Animated Series

Batman: The Animated Series is an American animated television series based on the DC Comics superhero Batman. It was developed by Bruce Timm and Eric Radomski. Produced by Warner Bros.t originally aired on Fox Kids from September 5, 1992, to September 15, 1995, with a total of 85 episodes.
